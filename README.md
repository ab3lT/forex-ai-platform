# Forex AI Platform

An AI-driven forex research and trading platform: hybrid ML/RL signal engine,
event-driven backtester, institutional-style risk engine, multi-broker execution
layer, FastAPI backend, Celery workers, React dashboard, and full deployment
scaffolding (Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD, Prometheus/Grafana).

> ⚠️ **Risk disclaimer.** Leveraged FX trading carries a high probability of
> loss; the large majority of retail accounts lose money. This software is for
> research and education. It defaults to **paper trading**. Nothing here is
> financial advice. Live trading requires your own validation, broker approval,
> and regulatory compliance.
